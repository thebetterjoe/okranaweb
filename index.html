How it works

Simple by design.
Powerful where it counts.

01
We sign the BAA before anything else
Before you log in, before you even see the product — we sign a Business Associate Agreement. That document is what makes it legal to use AI with patient or client data. It's standard on every plan, not locked behind enterprise tiers.
02
We configure it for your exact workflow
We spend a few hours learning how your practice actually works — your note format, your common document types, your intake process. Then we configure Okrana to match. You get something that already understands your practice on day one.
03
Your team is trained and live in 48 hours
We do a 2–4 hour training session with your staff, answer every question, and stay available by phone the first week. No complicated IT projects. No months of onboarding. Most practices are saving time by day two.
04
One person to call when anything needs changing
You won't get a ticket system or a support queue. You get a direct line to the person who built your setup. When something needs updating — a new template, a workflow change, a staff question — it gets handled same day.
